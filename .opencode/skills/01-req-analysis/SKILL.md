# 01-req-analysis Skill

**Objective:**
Perform Business Requirement Analysis based on the provided requirements in `req/business-requirement.md`.

**Instructions for the Agent:**
1. Locate and read the file `req/business-requirement.md`.
2. Identify the business purpose.
3. Identify actors (users/roles).
4. Identify entities and their attributes.
5. Identify relationships, cardinalities, and participation constraints.
6. Extract and explicitly list all business rules.
7. Explicitly document any assumptions made and any open questions.
8. Structure the output clearly with Markdown headings.

**Output:**
Create or update `outputs/01-business-req-analysis-G11.md` containing the full analysis.
